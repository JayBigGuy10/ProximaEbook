Chapter 13: Preface

Hi all, I've got a long post for you guys today! This week we're finishing up the last qualification flights to get started on the assembly of the MTV. We're really in the home stretch now before we can start final prep work for our Mars-shot. Our Crew Return Vehicle, much like it was in real life before it got cancelled, has faced lengthy delays but we are finally there. I can't wait for you all to meet all six CRVs we'll have on rotation. I wanna give a special huge thanks to Jay, who has once again blown me away with turning thoughts into reality, with his work on so many amazing vehicles - and so many more to come. I really encourage you all to check out Jay's work on twitter as well! I also want to thank a new contributor, Daylight, for their amazing amazing work on OPAV, a vehicle which seems to have really struck a chord with so many of you! Please give these two wonderful people all the love, they deserve it!

Anyway, let's go!
