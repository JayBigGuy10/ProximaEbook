Chapter 15: Annex

 

Hi everyone, don't know what happened yesterday, seems as though there has been some development with our friends in the Air Force Space Office. For today's image annex, I want to thank Jay who continues to dazzle with images of our first ship of the line, Minerva, in all of her free flight glory. I also wanna thank Mack Crawford for once again allowing me to use his images of the Boeing EELV, known in the Proxima universe as Helios. Coming up, she'll see preparedness for the first crewed test flight of the program, Olympus 1, in conjunction with her Lifeboat capsule and the Shuttle. It's a really exciting time, as we've got elements of old and new space coalescing into this dramatic range of ideas and practices in a world truly poised to make history.
