Chapter 15: Preface

This week I'd like to thank Jay, Mack Crawford and the NASA archive, who have kindly allowed me to use their amazing imagery to tell the story of Proxima. This week, we are exploring the launch market in the wake of the good ship Valiant and exploring how different routes could have been taken with an EELV style program in the face of additional Shuttle technology. We're also exploring the final bits of setup before we begin flying Olympus missions, and push higher further and faster than ever before. What an exciting time to be alive!
